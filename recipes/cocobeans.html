cocobeans

Description

Coconut in most stews is absolutely top-tier. Coconut beans go well with rice, chapati, and others we've not discovered yet.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups beans
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 bunch cilantro

Steps

  1. Boil beans in water until cooked.
  2. Add onions to pan and fry until browned. Add salt.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and Cook until paste like. Add in drained beans and fry several minutes.
  4. Add 1 cup water, cover, cook until water reduced in half.
  5. Cook 8-10 minutes until gravy has thickened.
  6. Garnish with cilantro before serving.
